# Task 4: Email Etiquette – Reflection

## Communication Scenario

One common real-world situation where poor digital 
communication causes serious problems is the misuse 
of social media by college students during placement 
season. There have been multiple reported cases where 
students who had already received job offers had those 
offers revoked after recruiters found them inappropriate 
posts on their public social media profiles. In one 
such scenario, a student posted a sarcastic comment 
mocking their future employer's work culture, not 
realizing their profile was public and visible to the 
company's HR team.

## What Could Have Been Done Differently

The student could have reviewed their privacy settings 
before the placement process began and ensured that 
personal opinions about companies were kept private 
or not posted at all. A simple habit of asking 
"would I be comfortable if my employer saw this?" 
before posting anything online could have prevented 
the situation entirely. This task taught me that 
professional digital communication is not limited 
to emails it extends to everything we post online.
